🧠 Theory Corner

Comedy Emotion Framework Discussion (6/8):

Four premise emotions: 难 (confused/stuck) · 怪 (that's weird) · 怕 (fear/anxiety) · 蠢 (stupid)

Key insight from Jesse: Anger ≠ 怪. 怪 is "I don't know why." Anger is "I know exactly why, and it shouldn't be this way." Your jokes work because you're not asking questions — you're exposing what everyone already knows but won't say.

This means your strongest material lives in a 5th category: 揭 (reveal/expose) — the comedy of saying the quiet part loud.
