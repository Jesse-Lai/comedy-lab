Premise: Fancy English Words Sound Like Coffee Orders

  • Chlamydia sounds like a girl's name. Hemorrhoid sounds like an asteroid. Constipation sounds like a government agency.
  • Chinese food names are the opposite — "红烧肉" literally translates to "red cooked meat." Sounds like a caveman describing dinner.callback

Premise: Can't Look Happy in China, Can't Look Sad in America

  • You can't look happy in China and you can't look sad in America. So when Americans ask me "how was your weekend?" — I have no idea which face to use.🔥

Premise: Indian English Comprehension Scale
