💡 Premises & Concepts

1. Corporate Politeness Is a Weapon

2. Working Abroad = Full-Time Translator (Of Yourself)

3. The English-Speaking Privilege (Great Wall Bit)

  • In China, speaking English makes you sound smart. My theory: the internet only imports Elon and Jensen Huang saying smart things. The idiots don't get subtitled. The wall filters stupidity out. A great wall.PREMISE
  • But it's getting better — not because China is more open. It's because Trump videos are getting imported now.

4. Chinese Racism (Regional Edition)

5. Company = Abusive Relationship

  • Sometimes I feel like a domestic violence victim — I think the company is nice when it's not hitting me.

6. Zoo = Human Nature Exhibit

  • Chinese tourists look at animals and discuss recipes. "Can you eat this?" is not even the question — edible is the default. The question is steamed or braised.
  • You can map human aesthetics by sound level at the zoo. The cuter the animal, the louder the crowd. The reptile house is the quietest place on earth.
